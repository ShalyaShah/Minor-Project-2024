Privacy Policy

Your data is safe and secure with GoTrip

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This policy explains how GoTrip collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, contact number, etc.) provided during registration or booking.
  • Payment details processed securely via third-party providers.
  • Device, browser, and IP address data for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings and provide customer support.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To send booking confirmations, updates, and promotional offers (opt-in only).

4. Data Sharing & Security

We never sell your data. Information may be shared with trusted partners (e.g., hotels or payment providers) strictly for booking fulfillment. We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to personalize your experience, remember preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at our support page.

7. Updates to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. All changes will be posted here with a revised date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us.
