Frequently Asked Questions

We’re here to help you with answers to common queries

🧾 How do I make a hotel booking?

You can search your destination, select a hotel, choose check-in/out dates, and complete payment online on GoTrip.

💸 Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, go to your bookings page. Depending on the hotel's policy, cancellation or modification may be free or involve a fee.

🔐 Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. All payments are processed via secure, encrypted gateways to protect your personal data.

⏱ When will I receive booking confirmation?

Instantly! A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email ID once the booking is complete.

📧 What if I don’t receive the confirmation email?

Please check your spam or junk folder. If not found, contact our support team right away.

🧳 Do you offer travel packages or only hotel bookings?

Currently we focus on hotel bookings, but stay tuned—curated travel packages are coming soon!
